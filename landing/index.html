How do I schedule my appointment?

You can easily schedule an appointment through our online appointment portal or by calling our appointment line at +639669382746. Choose your preferred doctor, date, and time, and we'll send you a confirmation.

What information do I need to provide when booking an appointment?

To ensure a smooth booking process, please have your full name, date of birth, insurance information (if applicable), and a brief description of your reason for the appointment ready.

Can I schedule an appointment for someone else?

Yes, you can schedule an appointment for a family member or another individual. Please provide their information as requested during the booking process.

How far in advance can I book an appointment?

Appointment availability varies by department and physician. Typically, you can book appointments several weeks in advance. For urgent needs, please call our appointment line to check for immediate availability.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment. Please do so at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any cancellation fees. You can manage your appointments through the online portal or by calling our appointment line.

Can I choose a specific doctor when booking?

Yes, you can select your preferred doctor during the booking process. If your chosen doctor is unavailable, we'll offer alternative options.
